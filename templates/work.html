My Career

New York University

In 2007, I moved to New York to study political science and studio art. I explored my love of media and enternmainment, working as a content management intern at Entertainment Weekly and a digital marketing intern at RaspberryRed (now DXagency).

Yahoo!

After graduating in 2011, I went to Yahoo! to work as a product manager on the publishing products platform. While there I realized my passion for JavaScript and started coding on the side.

AppAcademy

In January 2013, I enrolled in AppAcademy's 9 week Ruby on Rails course to hone my web development skills. I stayed on for the next cohort to work as a TA and help spread the coding love.

Vimeo

From spring 2013 to spring 2014, I worked as an associate application engineer at Vimeo, focusing on the front end of our web application. I dabbled in PHP, got serious with our JavaScript and seized every opportunity to make stuff fly across the screen.

Gawker Media

Now I'm at Gawker Media, working on the front-end of our publishing platform, Kinja while also working on iOS prototypes for future content discovery/discussion apps.
